# STORYLINE v2 — 24 Clicks, 8-10 Minutes

## The Argument

Atlys owns the highest-trust moment in Indian travel (visa approval), and that moment is a wedge into owning the entire journey — if they move in the next 18 months.

Every screen either builds that argument or it doesn't belong.

---

## Structure

| Section | Screens | Clicks | Job |
|---------|---------|--------|-----|
| Hook | 1-2 | 3 | "The market is huge, Atlys is tiny" |
| Journey | 3-8 | 16 | "Here's what's broken and what Atlys uniquely fixes" |
| Strategy | 9-11 | 4 | "Here's the bet, the moat, and the path" |
| Close | 12 | 1 | "Here's the one line you'll remember" |
| **Total** | **12** | **24** | **~8-10 minutes** |

First half is emotional (feel the traveler's pain). Second half is analytical (see the numbers). The exec gets both, in that order, in under 10 minutes. Then you talk.

## What changed from STORY.md v1

| Change | Why |
|--------|-----|
| 57 clicks -> 24 clicks | Exec won't click 57 times through dialogue boxes |
| JourneyMapScene appears once, not 5 times | After the second time it's friction, not atmosphere |
| Phase scenes: 6 beats -> 3 clicks | World+pain, hook, Atlys play. One visual per click, not one sentence. |
| Beat 0 (persona thought) cut | Redundant with Beat 2 (anxiety). Merged into click 1. |
| Strategy scenes: no intro beats | "Three hypotheses. Scored honestly." wastes a click. The cards say that. |
| Analytical content: read-at-your-pace | Hypotheses and GTM are 1-click screens. Exec reads, not waits for typewriter. |
| Punchline: 25s -> 10s | By screen 12 they either get it or they don't |
| Each phase has unique background | Garden, airport, dark tarmac, city, home — already built, not used |
| Hooks are visuals, not labels | Visa tags change color. Screens go black. Door opens. Not "green rectangle with text." |
